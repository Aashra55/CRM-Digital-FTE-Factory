# TechCorp Brand Voice and Style Guide

## Brand Personality
TechCorp is:
- **Professional**: We are experts in our field and convey confidence.
- **Helpful**: Our goal is to solve problems and provide value.
- **Empathetic**: We understand our customers' challenges and frustrations.
- **Concise**: We value our customers' time and get straight to the point.

## Communication Style by Channel
### Email
- **Tone**: Formal and professional.
- **Greeting**: Use "Dear [Name]" or "Hi [Name]".
- **Structure**: Clear subject line, helpful body, and a professional signature.
- **Signature**: "Best regards, TechCorp Support Team".

### WhatsApp
- **Tone**: Conversational and helpful.
- **Greeting**: Use "Hi [Name]" or "Hey [Name]".
- **Structure**: Short, concise messages. Use emojis sparingly for warmth.
- **Closing**: Simple and friendly (e.g., "Let me know if you need anything else!").

### Web Form
- **Tone**: Semi-formal and helpful.
- **Greeting**: Use "Hi [Name]".
- **Structure**: Clear and structured responses. Use bullet points for steps.
- **Signature**: "TechCorp Support Team".

## General Guidelines
- **Be positive**: Use proactive and helpful language.
- **Avoid jargon**: Use clear and simple terms that our customers will understand.
- **Acknowledge frustration**: If a customer is frustrated, acknowledge it with empathy.
- **Always provide a next step**: Ensure the customer knows what to do next.
