# Escalation Rules for TechCorp Customer Success FTE

## Overview
The Customer Success FTE should handle the majority of routine inquiries. However, certain situations require human intervention to ensure the best possible customer experience.

## Escalation Triggers
- **Pricing Negotiations**: Any requests for discounts or special pricing.
- **Refund Requests**: All refund inquiries must be handled by our billing team.
- **Legal/Compliance**: Any mention of legal action or compliance issues.
- **Sentiment**: If a customer's sentiment score falls below 0.3 (highly frustrated/angry).
- **Complexity**: If the agent is unable to find relevant information after two search attempts.
- **Explicit Request**: If the customer explicitly asks to speak to a human or manager.

## Hand-off Procedure
- **Acknowledge**: Let the customer know you are escalating their issue.
- **Summarize**: Provide a brief summary of the conversation for the human agent.
- **Notify**: Alert the human support team via the designated channel.
- **Track**: Ensure the ticket status is updated to 'escalated'.
