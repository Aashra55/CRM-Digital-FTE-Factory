# TechCorp SaaS Company Profile

## About Us
TechCorp is a leading provider of cloud-based project management tools for mid-sized engineering teams. Our mission is to streamline developer workflows and enhance team collaboration through automated project tracking and smart integrations.

## Target Audience
- Mid-sized engineering teams (20-100 developers).
- Project managers and CTOs looking for better visibility into development velocity.
- Individual contributors who need to track their tasks and time efficiently.

## Core Products
- **TechCorp Core**: Our flagship project management platform.
- **TechCorp Insights**: An AI-driven analytics dashboard for development teams.
- **TechCorp Connect**: A suite of integrations for Slack, GitHub, and Jira.

## Key Values
- **Simplicity**: Our tools should be easy to use and intuitive.
- **Transparency**: We believe in open communication and visible progress.
- **Innovation**: We are constantly pushing the boundaries of what's possible with AI and automation.
